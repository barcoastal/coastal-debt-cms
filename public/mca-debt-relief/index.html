    Bankruptcy Questions? We Have Answers.

    Get the facts before making a decision

    Is debt settlement really better than filing bankruptcy?
    For most business owners, yes. Bankruptcy stays on your record for 7-10 years, can force you to close your business, and makes future financing nearly impossible. Debt settlement is private, faster, and lets you keep operating.
    Will this affect my credit like bankruptcy would?
    Settlement does impact credit temporarily, but most clients see their scores recover within 12-24 months. Bankruptcy damage lasts 7-10 years.
    What types of debt can you settle?
    We specialize in Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs), business lines of credit, and unsecured business loans. If you're being sued or facing daily/weekly ACH withdrawals, we can help.
    How long does the process take?
    Most cases settle in 3-6 months. Bankruptcy proceedings can drag on for 6-18 months or longer.
    Do I need a lawyer to file bankruptcy? What about settlement?
    Bankruptcy requires legal representation and court appearances. Debt settlement doesn't — we handle all negotiations directly with your creditors.
    What if I've already talked to a bankruptcy lawyer?
    Get a second opinion. Many business owners come to us after consulting bankruptcy attorneys and discover settlement saves them more money with less damage to their business.
